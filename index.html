Let's Play Othello!

If you haven't played before, keep reading for
the rules. Otherwise, let's get to it.

The object of Othello is to finish the game with more pieces on the board than your opponent. To do this, you and your opponent take turns placing your respective pieces on the board. After you place your piece, any of your opponent's pieces that are "sandwiched" between the piece you played and your other pieces get flipped. You can flip along horizontal, vertical and diagonal directions. You must be able to flip at least one piece for the move to be legal. Got it?
